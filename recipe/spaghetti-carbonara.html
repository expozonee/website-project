Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/2 pound pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the pancetta and cook until crispy, about 5 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and black pepper.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving 1 cup of the cooking water. Add the pasta to the skillet with the pancetta and toss to combine.
  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and add the egg mixture. Toss the pasta with the egg mixture, adding the reserved cooking water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve the pasta garnished with chopped parsley and additional cheese, if desired.
