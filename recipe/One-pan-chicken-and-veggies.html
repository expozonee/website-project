One-Pan Chicken and Veggies

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 pound baby red potatoes, halved
  • 1 pound green beans, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly oil a baking sheet or coat with nonstick spray.
  2. Place chicken, potatoes, and green beans in a single layer onto the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper, to taste.
  3. Place into oven and roast until the chicken is completely cooked through, reaching an internal temperature of 165°F, about 25-30 minutes.
  4. Then broil for 2-3 minutes, or until caramelized and slightly charred.
  5. Let rest for 3 minutes.
  6. Serve immediately.
