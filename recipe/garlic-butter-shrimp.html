Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  3. Remove shrimp from skillet and set aside.
  4. Add garlic to the skillet and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Add chicken broth, white wine, and lemon juice to the skillet.
  6. Bring to a simmer and cook until reduced by half.
  7. Stir in parsley and butter until melted.
  8. Return shrimp to skillet and toss to coat in sauce.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Serve immediately.
