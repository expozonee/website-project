Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes and cook until the garlic is golden brown.
  5. Drain the pasta and add it to the skillet.
  6. Add the parsley and Parmesan cheese and toss to combine.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
